About This Dashboard

This Business Intelligence dashboard visualizes restaurant data including sales metrics, online orders vs. table bookings across multiple cities. The visualizations are powered by FusionCharts and D3.js.

The data analysis helps restaurant management make informed decisions about resource allocation, marketing strategies, and operational improvements.
